About Me

Welcome to my digital space! I am a Computer Science student with a strong passion for web development and problem-solving. I enjoy exploring how technology can be used to create meaningful solutions that make life easier and more efficient. Over time, I have developed a keen interest in building responsive, user-friendly websites that not only look good but also provide a smooth experience. Constantly learning new technologies and improving my skills keeps me motivated to grow as a developer.

My technical skill set spans both frontend and backend development. On the frontend, I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React to design interfaces that are clean, functional, and engaging. On the backend, I use Python and MySQL to manage data, process logic, and ensure that applications run seamlessly. In addition to web development, I am also exploring areas like machine learning and cybersecurity, which allow me to broaden my technical expertise and understand how software can be applied to solve more complex problems. This balance between core development and advanced concepts helps me stay versatile in the ever-evolving tech space.

Beyond academics, I actively take on projects that push me to think critically and apply what I learn in practical ways. These projects not only strengthen my technical skills but also teach me about teamwork, time management, and problem-solving in real-world scenarios. I enjoy collaborating with others, sharing knowledge, and continuously challenging myself to improve.
